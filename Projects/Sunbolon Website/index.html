About SunBolon

We are a premier events company based in Geneva, specializing in energy conferences and networking events that drive innovation and collaboration.

Creating Impactful Energy Events Since 2015

SunBolon is dedicated to fostering meaningful conversations and partnerships in the energy sector. Our flagship event, the Geneva New Fuels Conference, has become a must-attend gathering for industry leaders, policymakers, and innovators.

We pride ourselves on creating events that not only inform but inspire action towards sustainable energy solutions. Our team brings together decades of experience in event management and deep industry knowledge.
