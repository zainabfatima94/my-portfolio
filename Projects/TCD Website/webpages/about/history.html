The Founding Vision

In 2013, Johnathan T. Michaels established TCD Capital with a clear mission: to create an investment firm that combined rigorous fundamental research with disciplined risk management to deliver superior long-term returns.

"We saw an opportunity to build something different - a firm where investment decisions would be driven by deep analysis rather than short-term market noise."
