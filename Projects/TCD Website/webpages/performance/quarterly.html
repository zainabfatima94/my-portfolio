Navigating Q1 Market Volatility

April 15, 2025

The first quarter of 2025 presented investors with significant challenges as geopolitical tensions and shifting interest rate expectations drove increased market volatility. Despite these headwinds, the TCD Opportunity Fund delivered a 0.7% return, outperforming the S&P 500 by 5.0% during the quarter.

Our performance was driven by strong stock selection in the technology and healthcare sectors, where our fundamental research identified companies with durable competitive advantages trading at attractive valuations. Our underweight position in interest-rate sensitive sectors also contributed to relative outperformance.

Key Performance Drivers

  • Technology Sector Picks +2.1%
  • Healthcare Holdings +1.4%
  • Underweight Financials +0.8%
  • Currency Hedges -0.3%

Market Outlook

"We remain cautiously optimistic about market opportunities in 2025. While volatility may persist, we're finding compelling valuations in high-quality companies that have been unduly punished by short-term market sentiment. Our focus remains on businesses with strong cash flows, competent management teams, and sustainable competitive advantages."

Johnathan Michaels Chief Investment Officer
