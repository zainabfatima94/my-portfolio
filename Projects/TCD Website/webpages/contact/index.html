Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about TCD Capital

TCD Capital primarily serves institutional investors including pension funds, endowments, foundations, family offices, and high-net-worth individuals. Our minimum investment requirement varies by fund but typically starts at $5 million for our flagship Opportunity Fund.

You can request a meeting by completing our contact form, emailing info@tcdcapital.com, or calling our New York office at +1 (212) 999-5433. Our investor relations team typically responds to meeting requests within 24 business hours.

Existing investors can access their portfolio information through our secure client portal. If you need login credentials or have technical difficulties, please contact our client services team at clientservices@tcdcapital.com.

Our investment strategies are designed with a 3-5 year horizon, though this varies by asset class and market conditions. We employ a patient capital approach that allows our investment theses to fully develop while actively managing risk.

We provide comprehensive quarterly reports to all investors, including detailed performance analysis, portfolio commentary, and market outlook. Additionally, we host quarterly webinars and publish monthly updates for our flagship funds.
