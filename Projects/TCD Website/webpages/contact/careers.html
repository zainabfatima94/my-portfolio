Life at TCD Capital

  • Flat organizational structure

Professional Development

Our Hiring Process

What to expect when you apply to TCD Capital

1

Application Review

Our recruiting team reviews all applications and selects candidates whose experience aligns with the position requirements.

Typically 1-2 weeks

2

Initial Screening

Selected candidates will have a 30-minute phone or video call with a member of our HR team to discuss the role and your qualifications.

30 minute call

3

Skills Assessment

Depending on the role, you may complete a case study, modeling test, or technical assessment to demonstrate your abilities.

1-2 hour exercise

4

Team Interviews

You'll meet with 3-5 team members across different levels for 45-minute interviews, either in-person or via video conference.

3-4 hours total

5

Final Decision

Our hiring team convenes to make a final decision. We aim to provide feedback within one week of your final interview.

1 week turnaround
