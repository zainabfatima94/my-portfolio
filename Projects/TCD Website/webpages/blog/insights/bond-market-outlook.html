While the fixed income market continues to face headwinds from monetary tightening, current yields now offer attractive entry points for disciplined investors. We recommend:

  1. Focus on quality - Prioritize issuers with strong balance sheets
  2. Manage duration risk - Stay in the 3-5 year sweet spot
  3. Diversify across sectors - Blend corporates, MBS, and municipals
  4. Maintain liquidity - Keep 5-10% in cash equivalents for flexibility

"The bond market selloff has created the most attractive yields in over a decade. Patient investors building laddered portfolios today will be rewarded over the full cycle."
