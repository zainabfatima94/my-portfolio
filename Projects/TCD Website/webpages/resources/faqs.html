Fund Basics

Our Opportunity Fund has a minimum investment of $25,000 for individual investors. For institutional investors and retirement accounts, the minimum is $100,000. These minimums help ensure we can maintain proper diversification across all investor accounts.

Learn more about our Opportunity Fund investment strategy.

Our investment team conducts formal portfolio reviews quarterly, with tactical adjustments made as market conditions warrant. We maintain continuous monitoring of all positions and will rebalance whenever our risk parameters indicate the need.

Our three-pillar approach combines:

  1. Quantitative screening of 200+ market indicators
  2. Fundamental analysis by our sector specialists
  3. Proprietary risk management algorithms

This methodology is detailed in our Investment Philosophy.
