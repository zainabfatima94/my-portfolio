Investor FAQs

Common questions about our funds

The minimum initial investment for our Opportunity Fund is $250,000 for individual investors. For institutional investors and retirement accounts, different minimums may apply. Please contact our investor relations team for specific details.

We provide quarterly performance reports within 30 days after each quarter end. Additionally, investors receive monthly statements with position-level details and can access real-time performance through our client portal.

Our standard redemption policy allows for quarterly redemptions with 45 days' notice. There is a 1% redemption fee for withdrawals within the first 12 months. Certain share classes may have different terms.
